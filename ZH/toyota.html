Partner/Client: Burrell Communications
Audience: Students & Alumni of Historically Black Colleges and Universities
Goal: Increase sales of Toyota Environmental Fleet
Problem: Low engagement among influential market segment (African-American Consumers).Insight: 90% of African-American professionals graduate from a Historically Black Colleges or University. Alumni Engagement critical opportunity. Traditional Advertising not reaching target.
Solution: Co-designed & managed Toyota’s flagship green campaign targeting African-Americans, “The Toyota Green Initiative.” In its fifth year, this regional tour includes a Celebrity Lecture Series, Community Service Events and Contests. The initiative engaged millions of students and alumni in beautification projects, music and celebratory activities centered in themes of environmental sustainability, food justice and social entrepreneurship.
